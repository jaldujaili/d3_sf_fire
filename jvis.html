Jordan's D3 Visualization

Interpretation

Number of Records axis represents the total amount of records for a quarter. Time difference axis is the difference between the call time and when help went on scene. It is also color coded by the top 4 call types.

Discussion

The idea is to show the response time for help to arrive on scene from when a call comes in. This was to see if there was any time discrepancy between different call types.
